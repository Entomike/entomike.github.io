Scarlet-bodied Wasp Moth 

Cosmosoma myrodora Dyar, 1907

Tiger Moth Family Arctiidae

 

Port O'Connor, Calhoun Co., TX
25 October 2006 (P Hockey)

 

Anahuac NWR, Chambers Co., TX
8 October 2006 (Janet Rathjen) 

Texas County Records for Cosmosoma myrodora

Plus Cameron and Hidalgo Counties in so. TX and Angelina and Liberty Counties in east TX

Range: 

Southeastern states, primarily in Florida but also (generally near the coast) from South Carolina to Texas.

Flight Period: April to December, most records September to November (Brou 2003)

Larval Host: Climbing Hempweed, Mikania scandens, family Asteraceae - USDA Plants Database

Remarks: Occurs abundantly at ultra-violet light traps in Louisiana (Brou 2003)

Similar Species: 

Cosmosoma festivum, which has only once been recorded in s. Florida and s. Texas. 
Cosmosoma teuthras
Other congeners occur south of the border.
