Pachybrachis of Texas - Family Chrysomelidae

There are 59 spp. of Pachybrachis in Texas (Riley et al. 2003) plus numerous more undescribed spp.
  Over 80 Pachybrachis spp. from Texas and surrounding states are shown here.
Seven spp. are possibly endemic to Texas.
Specimens primarily shot from the ERGC, OSUC, TAMUIC, TORC and the UTIC collections

Pachy identification is complicated by a generally high level of intraspecific variation in color,
and to some extent, in sculpture. Also, most species show strong sexual dimorphism. (Riley et al. 2002)

Pubescent - Yellow - Striped - Dark - Mottled - Rio Grande - Morpho spp. - Adjacent States - Need - References

Pachybrachis morosus Haldeman - e. US to AZ - on Oaks (Syn: haematodes)
   

Pachybrachis thoracicus Jacoby - CA-TX, NV / Mex. - short series reported from willow
 

Pachybrachis marmoratus Jacoby - AZ to w. TX, CO / Mex - several recorded on various Asteraceae
    

Pachybrachis brunneus Bowditch - CA-TX-CO-UT - short series coll'ed from Salix in AZ
     

Pachybrachis integratus Fall - CA to sw TX - asso. w/ willow (Salix, Salicaeae)
     

Pachybrachis xanti Crotch - CA to w. TX, UT / Mex. - saltbush - Atriplex (Chenopodiaceae) and creosote
 

Slightly pubescent, pale sp. from Brewster Co.

Yellow Species (Fall's Group B) - All have western distributions

Pachybrachis abdominalis (Say) - AZ-TX-MB-AB - recorded from multiple plant families
  

Pachybrachis arizonensis Bowditch - CA-TX, KS, UT - coll'ed from Salix
   

Pachybrachis caelatus LeConte - CA-TX-SD-ID - most records from Asteraceae
   

Pachybrachis diversus Fall - NM-TX-IL-SD, FL - Normally asso. w/ willow, Salix, but also on Baccharis and Heterotheca
 

Pachybrachis immaculatus Jacoby - CA-TX (IA, IL ?) / Mex. - on Parthenium hysterophorus (Asteraceae) in Mex.
 

Pachybrachis jacobyi Bowditch - CA-TX-SD-UT - Reported from several herbacious plant families.


Pachybrachis macronychus Fall - w. TX - Asso. w/ Mexican pinon pine
   

Pachybrachis mellitus Bowditch - CA-TX-UT-NV - hosts on Creosote Bush
 

Pachybrachis minor Bowditch - CA to w. TX / Mex. - Ericameria and Hymenoclea (Asteraceae) appear to be hosts
  

Pachybrachis mitis Fall  - Texas endemic? - pale, lightly blotched, ~2 mm - Illinois bundleflower (Desmanthus illinoensis, Fabaceae)
   

Pachybrachis nero Bowditch - AZ to w. TX - asso. w/ snakeweed - Gutierrezia spp. (Asteraceae)
   

Pachybrachis pusillus Bowditch - AZ to c. TX / Mex. - eyes bulge, nearly touch - possibly on mesquite
 

Pachybrachis umbraculatus Suffrian - AZ to w. TX / Mex. - one adult collected from Baccharis in AZ
   

Pachybrachis xantholucens Fall - NM to w. TX - beaten or swept from creosote bush - Larrea tridentata
 
Striped Species (Fall's Group D)

Pachybrachis bivittatus (Say) - CA-GA-QC-BC / Mex. - Hosts on Salix, Populus (Salicaceae)
   

Pachybrachis dubiosus LeConte - NM-TX-KS-CO / Mex. - Found feeding on Baccharis foliage.
Median thoracic stripe bifurcate in front, but does not tough the lateral stripes.
   

Pachybrachis nigricornis autolycus Fall - AZ-TX-IL-MT / adj. CAN - Large series coll'ed from Desmodium (Fabaceae)
 

Pachybrachis othonus (Say) - TX-GA-ME-ND / adj Can. - recorded from many plant families
 

Pachybrachis othonus pallidipennis Suffrian - TX-LA-SK-AB
 

Pachybrachis pawnee Fall - Great Plains - coll'ed from dock - Rumex (Polygonaceae)
   

Pachybrachis vau Fall - AZ-TX-SD-ID / AB - reported from var. Asteraceae
  

Pachybrachis viduatus (Fabricius) - TX-FL-NC - hosts on St. John's-Wort, Hypericum tenuifolium (Clusiaceae). only known TX record from Tyler, TX.
  

Pachybrachis virgatus LeConte - TX-MO-SD-CO - median stripe dilated, but not bifurcate anteriorly - single spmn coll'ed from Polygonum (Polygonaceae)
   

Dark Species (Fall's Group E)

Pachybrachis cruentus LeConte - Endemic to Texas - Adults on Live Oaks
  

Pachybrachis hector Fall - TX-KS - Coll'ed from blooming Acacia
    

Pachybrachis femoratus (Olivier) - TX-FL-NY-IN - Reported from a variety of hosts (commonly coll'ed in Alabama)

 Pachybrachis turgidicollis Fall - NM to w. TX - Type - brown with yellow marks, eyes close, stocky ('swollen neck')
      

Pachybrachis varians Bowditch - TX-AL-TN-OK
     

Pachybrachis wenzeli Fall - AZ to w. TX - Type - on oak (and Mimosa) based on just two spmns.
