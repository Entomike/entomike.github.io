Twin-shielded Leaf Beetle

Paranapiacaba connexa (J. L. LeConte, 1865)

Family Chrysomelidae, Subfamily Galerucinae, Tribe Luperini

 

Corpus Christi, Nueces County, Texas
January 5, 2007 (P Crispin)

Range: Texas, Mexico

Flight Period: Specimens from every month except October & December in the TAMUIC.

Host: This species feeds on members of the gourd family Cucurbitaceae

Similar Species: 

This genus includes more than 60 described species. (Arnett et al. 2002).
However only two range north of Mexico:

Paranapiacaba connexa (J. L. LeConte, 1865) - TX / Mex.
Paranapiacaba tricincta (Say, 1824) - AZ, Co, KS, NM, SD, TX, UT, WY / Mex.
