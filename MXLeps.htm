This book illustrates all species and most "subspecies" of Mexican butterflies in these families in color, provides distribution maps for each taxon, and details distributional records and their sources. For a complete review of the book, see: 

Warren, A. D. 1999. J. Lepid. Soc. 52(3):342-343.
