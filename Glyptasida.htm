Carved Darkling Beetle

Glyptasida sp.

Family Tenebrionidae, Subfamily Tentyriinae, Tribe Asidini

 

Big Bend National Park, Brewster Co., TX
October 13, 2004 (M Stangeland & K Davis)

Similar Species: There are 13 species of Glyptasida in the south central western United States. Genus is in need of revision.

Photo: Glyptasida sordidus - Colorado Plateau Museum of Arthropod Biodiversity
