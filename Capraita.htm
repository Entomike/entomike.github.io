Charlie Brown Flea Beetle

Capraita sexmaculata (Illiger)

Family Chrysomelidae, Subfamily Galerucinae, Tribe Alticini

 

Woburn, Middlesex Co., Massachusetts
July 5, 2005 (Tom Murray)

State Records for Capraita sexmaculata

State Record Source: (Riley et al. 2003)

Range: Texas north to South Dakota, east to Massachusetts, south to Florida (Riley et al. 2003).

Larval Hosts: 

Apparently associated with Oleaceae. It has been recorded from Chionanthus virginicus, Fraxinus americana, Fraxinus berlandieriana, and Forestiera ligustrina (Clark et al. 2004).

Adult Activity: April 25 to June 25 in Alabama based on 16 specimens (Balsbaugh & Hays 1972).

Similar Species: 

There are 60 species of Capraita in North, Central and South America (Scherer 1983).
Sixteen spp. occur north of Mexico (Riley et al. 2003).
