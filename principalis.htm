Type Locality: Oaxaca

Taxonomy: Orig. Comb: Eupyra principalis Walker [1865]

Note: Druce (1884) reported that the only specimen known to him was the type in the British Museum.

Similar Species: Chrysocale regalis (Boisduval, 1836)

Biography: Francis Walker (1809 - 1874) - Wikipedia
