Weevils of Texas - Superfamily Curculionoidea

Texas Beetle Resources

There are some 1218 weevils spp. in Texas (E.G. Riley, pers. comm. 2017)
Some 505 spp., or over 40% of the Texas total are illustrated here.

Many many thanks to Robert Anderson, Tom Atkinson, Vassili Belov, Horace Burke, Wayne Clark, Henry Hespenheide, Charles O'Brien, Jens Prena, Edward Riley
and BugGuide for much needed assistance over the years, but any mistakes below are my own.
