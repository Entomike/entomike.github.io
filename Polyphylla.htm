Lined June Beetle

Polyphylla hammondi complex

Family Scarabaeidae, Subfamily Melolonthinae, Tribe Melolonthini

 

(males have expanded antennal clubs)

Utley, Bastrop Co., TX
July 16, 2006 (Brush Freeman)
At Blacklight

Det. and Comment per Ed Riley, "This "species" seems to be restricted to the Colorado River flood plane and also the upper tributaries of that river."

Similar Species: 

 There are 29 species of Polyphylla for North America (Arnett, et.al. 2002).

Texas Polyphylla species: Per Riley & Wolfe (2003)
