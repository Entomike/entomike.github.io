Return to Texas Entomology - Compiled by Mike Quinn

 

TX-Butterfly is a listserv initiated in February of 2001 for the discussion of butterflies, moths and other entomological issues in Texas and surrounding areas such as northern Mexico. This list is hosted on  the University of Houston servers. It's sponsored by David Sarkozi as a privilege of his being a staff member at the U of H. In addition to David, the other list owners are owners are Melinda Parmer and Mike Quinn, they can always be reached at tx-butterfly-request@listserv.uh.edu. 

As of March 26, 2020 there were approximately 230 subscribers to TX-Butterfly.

  • If you would like to subscribe to TX-Butterfly, send an email to:

LISTSERV@LISTSERV.UH.EDU

Put the following command in the body of your email:

Subscribe TX-Butterfly your name
(For example: Subscribe TX-Butterfly Carl Linné)

Leave the email subject line blank and turn off any automatic signatures that you may have.

-----------------------------------------------------------------------------

  • You may also subscribe/unsubscribe on-line from this link:

https://listserv.uh.edu/cgi-bin/wa?SUBED1=TX-BUTTERFLY&A=1

-----------------------------------------------------------------------------

  • Once subscribed, to post a message send it to this address:

TX-BUTTERFLY@LISTSERV.UH.EDU

Please send all messages in plain text. Also, please don't send attachments to the list.

-----------------------------------------------------------------------------

  • Here's a live link to the most recent archives:

http://birdingonthe.net/mailinglists/TEXB.html

-----------------------------------------------------------------------------

  • Click below to search the entirety of the archives:

https://listserv.uh.edu/cgi-bin/wa?REPORT&z=4&1=TX-BUTTERFLY&L=TX-BUTTERFLY

  • Click below to view all archives:

https://listserv.uh.edu/cgi-bin/wa?A0=TX-BUTTERFLY

-----------------------------------------------------------------------------

  • To unsubscribe, put the following command:

SIGNOFF TX-BUTTERFLY 

 in the body of an email and send it to:

LISTSERV@LISTSERV.UH.EDU

-----------------------------------------------------------------------------

  • When going on vacation, send the following command:

SET TX-BUTTERFLY NOMAIL

To resume mail service, send the following command:

SET TX-BUTTERFLY MAIL

Send all commands to LISTSERV@LISTSERV.UH.EDU

  • Politeness is greatly appreciated on TX-Butterfly!!!
