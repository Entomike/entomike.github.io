Compiled by Michael D. Overton

Resident Naturalist for Frio Country Resorts in Uvalde, Co.
Concan, TX - mdoverton@stx.rr.com

 The following list of 303 taxa were compiled over four days in the field 
in southern Tamaulipas & San Luis Potosi, October 2006

Contact Mike for future Mexico trip information

Mike Overton has visited northeastern Mexico more than 40 times and led over two dozen tours in the area. Since 1996 he's worked as a self-employed naturalist sharing his love of butterflies and birds with fellow enthusiasts at festivals, workshops and conventions across the United States. He's a voting member of the Iowa Ornithologists' Union's Iowa Bird Records Committee, Field Trip Coordinator for the Texas Ornithological Society, Research Collector for Universidad de Colima and for the C. P. Gillette Museum of Arthropod Diversity of Colorado State University and Technical Advisor and Names Committee Secretary for Mariposas Mexicanas.
