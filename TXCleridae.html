Checkered Beetles of Texas - Family Cleridae

139 species of clerids have been recorded from Texas (E.G. Riley, pers. comm. 2022)
The following thumbnails illustrate over 70% of the Texas total.

Speciemens primarily shot at the TAMUIC and the UTIC insect collections.

Cymatodera -- Phyllobaenus -- Enoclerus -- Trichodes -- Pelonides -- Chariessa -- Necrobia

Subfamily Thaneroclerinae

    • Ababa tantilla (LeConte) - e. US to Mex.

Subfamily Tillinae

    • Araeodontia - 2 Texas spp. - w. TX to s. CA

    • Cymatodera - 30 Texas spp. - e NA to CA

== - Cymatodera balteata LeConte 1854 - mostly Waco to Rio Grande, note: elongated reddish pronotum, main elytral band mostly straight edged, upper band often broken or even absent
= - Cymatodera sirpata Horn 1885 - common, Red River to Rio Grande, note: band edges not highly undulate, pale elytral apices
 = - Cymatodera undulata (Say 1825) - common, e. US to AZ, note: highly undulate edges of bands, dark elytral apices.
  - Cymatodera wolcotti Barr 1950 - uncommon, se US, very similar to, and possibly a synonym of, C. undulata
=== - Cymatodera puncticollis Bland 1863 - AZ to c. TX
