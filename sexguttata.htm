Six-spotted Tiger Beetle

Cicindela sexguttata Fabricius

Family Carabidae, Subfamily Cicindelinae

 

Camp Ben McCullough, Hays Co., Texas
March 25, 2006 (Shawn Ashbaugh, Scott Young)

Range: Eastern North America, to east Texas

Similar Species: There are some 57 species and subspecies of Cicindelinae in Texas (E.G. Riley, pers. comm.)
