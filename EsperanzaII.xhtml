Important Beetle Type Localities in the
Brownsville Area of Cameron County, Texas

"Esperanza Ranch", "San Tomas/St. Thomas" and "Brownsville" are the type localities for many beetles species described from Cameron County.
(No one described more beetles, or indeed other insects, from South Texas than did Charles Schaeffer.)
