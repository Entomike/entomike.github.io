Crustacea - Crayfishes, etc.

Cambaridae - Northern Hemisphere Crayfishes

  • Cambarellus (Pandicambarus) ninae Hobbs, 1950 - Aransas, Calhoun, Refugio, and Victoria Co.
  • Fallicambarus (Fallicambarus) devastator Hobbs and Whiteman, 1987 - Texas Prairie Crayfish - Angelina Co.
  • Fallicambarus (Fallicambarus) macneesei (Black, 1967) - Brazoria Co..
  • Orconectes (Hespericambarus) maletae Walls, 1972 - Upshur Co.
  • Procambarus (Capillicambarus) brazoriensis Albaugh, 1975 - Brazoria Co.
  • Procambarus (Girardiella) kensleyi Hobbs, 1990 - Angelina, Jasper, Newton, Panola, San Augustine, Trinity and Tyler Co.
  • Procambarus (Ortmannicus) nechesae Hobbs, 1990 - Angelina and Jasper Co.
  • Procambarus (Girardiella) nigrocinctus Hobbs, 1990 -  Angelina and Jasper Co.
  • Procambarus (Ortmannicus) nueces Hobbs and Hobbs, 1995 - Atascosa Co.
  • Procambarus (Girardiella) steigmani Hobbs, 1991 - Collin Co.
  • Procambarus (Ortmannicus) texanus Hobbs, 1971 - Bastrop Co.

  • Tamaulipan Clubtail (Gomphurus gonzalezi) - Starr, Hidalgo, Cameron Co., TX
  • Ringed Forceptail (Phyllocycla breviphylla) - Starr, Hidalgo, Cameron Co., TX

Libellulidae - Skimmers

  • Red-mantled Skimmer (Libellula gaigei) - Hidalgo Co., TX

  • Mexican Scarlet-tail (Planiplax sanguiniventris) - Hidalgo Co., TX
  • Arch-tipped Glider (Tauriphila argo) - Hidalgo Co., TX

  • Cream-tipped Damsel (Leptobasis melinogaster) - RGV, plus Kleberg, Dimmit, and Brewster Counties

  • Caribbean Yellowface (Neoerythromma cultellatum) - Hidalgo & Cameron Co., TX, plus s. FL
  • Marsh Firetail (Telebasis digiticollis) - Hidalgo & Cameron Co.

Orthoptera - Grasshoppers

Family Tettigoniidae (Katydids)

    Meadow katydids - Subfamily Conocephalinae

  • Brownsville Meadow Katydid (Conocephalus resacensis) - Cameron Co.

    False katydids - Subfamily Phaneropterinae

  • Uhler's virtuoso katydid (Amblycorypha uhleri) - Central Texas
  • Walking thread-legged katydid (Arethaea ambulator) - Central and North-Central Texas
  • Rio Grande thread-legged katydid (Arethaea phantasma) - Along Rio Grande from Brewster to Cameron Counties.
  • Spoon-tail short-winged katydid (Dichopetala catinata) - Central Texas
  • Gladiator Short-winged Katydid (Obolopteryx gladiator) - South Texas
  • Thumb-bearing short-winged katydid (Dichopetala pollicifera) - Cameron Co.
  • Seever's short-winged katydid (Dichopetala seeversi) - One Record in Central Texas
  • Texas angle-wing (Microcentrum minus) - South Texas

    True katydids - Subfamily Pseudophyllinae

  • Chisos katydid (Paracyrtophyllus excelsus) - Brewster Co.

    Shield-backed katydids - Subfamily Tettigoniinae

  • Daedalus Shieldback (Pediodectes daedalus) - Two  records, sw TX
  • Mitchell's Shieldback (Pediodectes mitchelli) - One record, sw TX
  • Pratt's Shieldback (Pediodectes pratti) - One record, s. TX

Family Gryllidae (Crickets)

    Anomalous crickets - Subfamily Pentacentrinae

  • Belfrage’s cricket (Trigonidomimus belfragei) - North-Central Texas

Coleoptera - Beetles

Carabidae - Ground Beetles

    Tiger Beetles - Subfamily Cicindelinae

  • Cazier's Tiger Beetle - Cicindela cazieri - Starr Co.
  • Lime-headed Tiger Beetle - Cicindela chlorocephala smythi - Cotype - 80 specimens collected on SPI in 1912, not seen since...
  • Black Sky Tiger Beetle - Cicindela nigrocoerulea subtropica - Cameron & Hidalgo Counties
  • Large Grassland Tiger Beetle - Cicindela obsoleta neojuvenilis - Maverick Co. south to Hidalgo Co.
