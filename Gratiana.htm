Eggplant Tortoise Beetle

Gratiana pallidula (Boheman)

Family Chrysomelidae, Subfamily Cassidinae, Tribe Cassidini

 

Chattahoochee River NRA
Abbots Bridge, Fulton Co., Georgia
September 3, 2003 (Troy Bartlett)

Texas County Records for Gratiana pallidula

County Record Data from E.G. Riley, Dec. 2005

State Records for Gratiana pallidula

Data from Riley et al. 2003

Host: Solanum sp., family Solanaceae (Clark et al. 2004)

Similar Species: There are seven species of Gratiana in South America. Only Gratiana pallidula occurs in the US (Arnett et al. 2002). 

Taxonomy:

Gratiana pallidula (Boheman, 1854)
        Cassida pallidula Boheman, 1854
        Cassida texana Crotch, 1873
