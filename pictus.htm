Ak Grasshopper

Poekilocerus pictus (Fabricius)

Orthoptera: Acrididae, Pyrogomorphinae

The normal host is Calotrope (Calotropis procera)

Other common names: rubber bush, apple of Sodom, mudar, madar, king's crown, roostertree

Asclepiadaceae or "Ak Family"

nr. Ujjain , Madhya Pradesh, India
