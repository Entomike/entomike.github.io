Cottonwood Leaf Beetle

Chrysomela scripta Fabricius 

Family Chrysomelidae, Subfamily Chrysomelinae, Tribe Chrysomelini

cf: Chrysomela knabi

 

Juno Beach, Palm Beach Co., Florida
July 2, 2004 (Tony DiTerlizzi)

State Records for Chrysomela scripta

State Record Data per Riley et al. 2003

Range: All of US except west coast states, NV and AZ (Riley et al. 2003).

Hosts: This species feeds on Salix spp., and Populus spp., Salicaceae (Clark et al. 2004).

Adult Activity: April 1 to September 18 in Alabama based on 92 specimens (Balsbaugh & Hays 1972).

Similar Species: 

Sixteen species of this Holarctic genus occur in the US and Canada (Arnett et al. 2002).

Texas Taxa: (per Riley et al. 2003)

Chrysomela interrupta Fabricius 
Chrysomela knabi Brown
Chrysomela scripta Fabricius
Chrysomela texana (Schaeffer)
