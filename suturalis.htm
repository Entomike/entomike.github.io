Ragweed Leaf Beetle

Zygogramma suturalis (Fabricius)

Family Chrysomelidae, Subfamily Chrysomelinae, Tribe Chrysomelini

cf: Zygogramma heterothecae, Zygogramma exclamationis

 

Harvard, Worcester Co., Massachusetts
July 18, 2004 (Tom Murray)

State Records for Zygogramma suturalis

State Record Data per Riley et al, 2003

Range: 

Eastern United States, west to Great Plains states, plus Colorado, New Mexico and the Canadian Provinces of Ontario and Quebec. 
Introduced into Eurasia (Riley et al. 2003).

Adult Activity: June 13 to October 12 in Alabama based on 43 specimens (Balsbaugh & Hays 1972).

Hosts: Feeds on the following species of Ragweed, Asteraceae (Clark et al. 2004)

Ambrosia artemisiifolia L.
Ambrosia psilostachya DC.
Ambrosia trifida L. 

Similar Species: 

This genus contains about 100 species distributed throughout much of North and South America, with 13 species occurring north of Mexico, primarily in the western half of the continent (Arnett et al. 2002).
