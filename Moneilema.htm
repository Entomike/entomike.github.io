Cactus Longhorn Beetle

Moneilema sp.

Family Cerambycidae, Subfamily Lamiinae, Tribe Moneilemini

 

(on Opuntia)

Hidalgo Co., TX (M Quinn)

Similar Species: There are 20 species of Moneilema, six occur from Texas and western North America to Mexico. 

Weblinks: Rearing Moneilema armatum - Elytra and Antenna (Invertebrates-Magazine)
