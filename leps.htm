Major Texas Regional Lepidoptera Checklists

Knudson, E. & C. Bordelon. 2003. Pub. 1: Checklist of the Audubon Sabal Palm Grove Sanctuary.  Texas Lepidoptera Survey, Houston. (treats 785 species, 10 color plates).

Knudson, E. & C. Bordelon. 2003. Pub. 2: Checklist of the Big Thicket National Preserve. Texas Lepidoptera Survey, Houston. (treats 1737 species, 15 color plates).

Knudson, E. & C. Bordelon. 2003. Pub. 3: Checklist of Big Bend National Park. Texas Lepidoptera Survey, Houston. (treats 1300 species, 12 color plates). 

Knudson, E. & C. Bordelon. 2003. Pub. 4: Checklist of Guadalupe Mountains National Park. Texas Lepidoptera Survey, Houston. (treats 1300 species, 12 color plates).  

Knudson, E. & C. Bordelon. 2003. Pub. 5: Checklist of the Caprock Canyonlands. Texas Lepidoptera Survey, Houston. (treats 1000 species, 12 color plates). 

Knudson, E. & C. Bordelon. 2003. Pub. 6: Checklist of the Lepidoptera of Texas. Texas Lepidoptera Survey, Houston. (not illustrated). 

Knudson, E. & C. Bordelon. 2003. Pub. 6a: Checklist of the Lepidoptera of Texas. Texas Lepidoptera Survey, Houston.  (12 color plates not in other regional checklists). 

Knudson, E. & C. Bordelon. (in prep). Pub. 6b: Checklist of Texas Lepidoptera. Texas Lepidoptera Survey, Houston. (85 color plates). 

Knudson, E. & C. Bordelon. 2003. Pub. 7: Checklist of Davis Mountains, TX. Texas Lepidoptera Survey, Houston. (treats 1100 species, 10 color plates).  

Knudson, E. & C. Bordelon. 2003. Pub. 8: Checklist of the Texas Hill Country. Texas Lepidoptera Survey, Houston. (treats 1900 species, 10 color plates).  

Bordelon, C. & E. Knudson. 2003. Pub. 9: Checklist of the Lower Rio Grande Valley, Part 1: Butterflies. (treats all species, 20 plates). Texas Lepidoptera Survey, Houston.
